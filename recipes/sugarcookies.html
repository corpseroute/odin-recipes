Sugar Cookies

Delcious and Made of Sugar

Ingredience

You will need:
  • 2 3/4th cupes all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup white sugar

Steps:

  1. Look up what it means to preheat oven and then preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda and baking powder in a small bowl.
  3. Beat butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar in a large bowl with an electric mixer or by hand if you want to die; sit in egg and vanilla extract and gradually blend flour mixture into butter mix.
  4. Roll dough into walnut sized balls and toss in 1/4 cup sugar. Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet because they're not gay.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 8-9 minutes. Let stand on cookie sheet or eat them immediately to melt your face off.
