Peanut Butter Cookies

You want cookie?

Yes. Delcious, crunchy cookie. You will make and be satisfied. Share with dog!

Ingredience

You will need:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter (yummy)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar (where is she going?)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Cream the butters and sugars together in a bowl; beat in eggs.
  2. In different bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; stir in butter sauce. Put dough in fridge for hour.
  3. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheet. Flatten each ball with a fork to make a crisscross pattern. Bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees for about 10 minutes.
  4. Try to eat them too soon and melt your face off.
